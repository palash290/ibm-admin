Steps to OPERATE your Mortgage Minimizer

Instructions

One week after making the annual premium payment into your policy(s), take the maximum available policy loan(s) and use that money to make an early payment on your mortgage principal.

Dollar for dollar that amount will become available in the HELOC. Withdraw that amount and use it to pay off your other debts as directed below.

Note, you are not making any additional payments, only changing who receives them. You borrowed money from the insurance carrier who placed a lien on your policy. Every payment to your policy loan reduces the lien, and increases the amount immediately available to be used for another purpose. For each credit and loan item paid out, the payments you were making will now be made to the policy loan. For example, if you were making a monthly payment towards your credit card, now that it is paid off, that same payment must be paid towards the policy loan.

  • From year 3 to year 7

    Now that all credit and loan items are paid, put the full available policy loan annually towards the mortgage until it's paid in full.
